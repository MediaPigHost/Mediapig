Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide IPv6?

At the minute we have not implemented IPv6. All of our kit is IPv6 ready and we are looking to go ahead with this very soon!

Can users run their own kernels, or are these externally provided?

There are no restrictions for users to run their own kernels.

Do you provide access to the serial console, or VNC, if the user makes a firewalling mistake?

We provide access to a remote console access via HTML5 Canvas. That's available in your account immediately after purchase.

Where are your machines hosted, and do you own them or merely resell?

Currently our machines are owned by Hivelocity in the United States but we do have around the clock support with them.

Can users configure custom reverse DNS for their IPs?

Yes, we allow users to configure custom reverse DNS for their IPs.

Do you offer backups?

We can provide backups. At the minute these need to be individually requested but we want to automate this in an upcoming release.

What virtualization technology do you use?

Our disk space is using RAID 10 SATA and we use KVM Virtualisation.
